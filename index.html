Service Proclaim and Payment Terms

Service Proclaim

At Pathomics, we are committed to delivering high-quality pathology services with precision, reliability, and efficiency. Our team of experienced pathologists and technicians utilizes state-of-the-art technology to provide accurate and timely results for your research and clinical needs.

Quotation Process

All services are provided based on customized quotations tailored to your specific requirements. To request a quote, please contact us via email with detailed information about your project, including service type, sample number, and timeline.

Payment Terms

  • A 50% deposit is required to initiate the project, with the remaining balance due upon completion of services.
  • Payment methods include bank transfer, credit card, and purchase orders from established institutions.
  • For academic institutions and non-profit organizations, special pricing may be available upon request.
  • All invoices are due within 30 days of receipt unless otherwise specified in the service agreement.
  • Rush services are available for an additional fee, subject to laboratory capacity.

Confidentiality and Data Security

We maintain strict confidentiality of all client information and research data. Our secure data management systems ensure the protection of your valuable intellectual property and comply with relevant data protection regulations.

Sample Requirements

Specific sample preparation guidelines will be provided upon project initiation to ensure optimal results. Improper sample preparation may affect service quality and turnaround time.

Turnaround Time

Standard turnaround times vary by service type and sample volume. Estimated completion times will be included in your quotation. We strive to meet all deadlines while maintaining our high-quality standards.
