Our Services

Pathomics offers a comprehensive range of advanced pathology services to support your research and clinical needs.

Histology

Our histology services include tissue processing, embedding, sectioning, and staining with a wide range of histochemical stains. We provide high-quality tissue preparations with rapid turnaround times and expert interpretation.

Learn More

Immunohistochemistry

Our immunohistochemistry (IHC) services detect specific antigens in tissue sections using validated antibodies. We offer single and multiplex IHC with quantitative analysis for biomarker expression in various tissue types.

Learn More

Multiplex Immunofluorescence

Our multiplex immunofluorescence technology allows simultaneous detection of multiple biomarkers in a single tissue section, providing spatial context and cellular relationships for comprehensive tissue analysis.

Learn More

Tissue Microarray

Our tissue microarray (TMA) services enable high-throughput analysis of multiple tissue samples on a single slide. We offer custom TMA construction, sectioning, and staining for efficient biomarker screening and validation.

Learn More

Antibody Validation for IHC

We provide comprehensive antibody validation services for immunohistochemistry applications, ensuring specificity, sensitivity, and reproducibility of your antibodies across various tissue types and fixation conditions.

Learn More

Digital Slide Scanning

Our digital slide scanning services convert glass slides into high-resolution digital images for remote viewing, sharing, and analysis. We offer whole slide imaging with rapid turnaround times and secure data storage options.

Learn More

Imaging Analysis

Our advanced imaging analysis services utilize state-of-the-art algorithms and machine learning to quantify biomarker expression, cell populations, and tissue architecture from digital pathology images with high accuracy and reproducibility.

Learn More

Positive Control Tissue Slides

We provide a comprehensive collection of positive control tissue slides for various biomarkers and applications. Our quality-controlled slides ensure reliable controls for your immunohistochemistry and research applications.

Learn More
