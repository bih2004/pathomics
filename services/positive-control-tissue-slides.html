Positive Control Tissue Slides

Quality-controlled tissue slides for reliable controls in immunohistochemistry and research applications

Overview

Positive control tissue slides are essential components of quality assurance in immunohistochemistry (IHC) and other tissue-based assays. These slides contain tissues with known expression patterns of specific biomarkers, providing reliable references to validate staining procedures and antibody performance.

At Pathomics, we offer a comprehensive collection of high-quality positive control tissue slides for various biomarkers and applications. Our control slides are meticulously prepared, quality-controlled, and validated to ensure consistent and reliable performance in your research and diagnostic applications. Whether you need standard controls for routine IHC or specialized controls for challenging biomarkers, our extensive collection provides the quality assurance you need for confident results.

Our Positive Control Tissue Slide Services Include

  • Standard Control Slides: Well-characterized tissue slides for common biomarkers and staining applications.
  • Multi-Tissue Control Slides: Slides containing multiple tissue types for comprehensive control assessment.
  • Custom Control Slides: Tailored control slides for specific biomarkers and applications.
  • Tissue Microarray Controls: TMA-format controls with multiple tissues on a single slide.
  • Cell Line Controls: Slides prepared from cell lines with defined biomarker expression profiles.
  • Xenograft Controls: Slides from xenograft models with characterized biomarker expression.
  • Graded Expression Controls: Slides with tissues showing varying levels of biomarker expression.
  • Validated Controls: Extensively validated controls for critical biomarkers.
  • Digital Control Images: Digital reference images of expected staining patterns.

Control Slide Formats

We offer several formats of positive control tissue slides to meet your specific requirements:

Single Tissue Controls

Standard slides containing a single tissue type known to express the biomarker of interest. Ideal for routine quality control and antibody validation.

Multi-Tissue Controls

Slides containing multiple tissue types on a single slide, allowing for assessment of biomarker expression across different tissues. Useful for evaluating antibody specificity and cross-reactivity.

Tissue Microarray Controls

TMA-format slides containing multiple tissue cores on a single slide. Provides comprehensive control assessment with minimal reagent consumption.

Graded Expression Controls

Slides containing tissues with varying levels of biomarker expression (negative, weak, moderate, strong). Useful for assessing staining sensitivity and dynamic range.

Cell Line Controls

Slides prepared from cell lines with defined biomarker expression profiles. Provides consistent and reproducible controls for challenging biomarkers.

Custom Controls

Tailored control slides designed to meet your specific requirements. Can include combinations of tissues, cell lines, and expression levels.

Careful selection of tissues with consistent and well-characterized biomarker expression.

3

Block Assessment

Evaluation of tissue blocks for quality, representation, and biomarker expression.

4

Sectioning
