Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you process samples?

Our standard turnaround time varies by service type. Routine histology and IHC typically require 3-5 business days, while more complex services like multiplex immunofluorescence or AI-powered analysis may take 7-10 business days. Rush processing is available for an additional fee.

Do you accept international samples?

Yes, we accept samples from international clients. Please contact us for specific shipping instructions and import/export requirements for your country.

How should I prepare and ship my samples?

Sample preparation requirements vary by service type. Please contact our laboratory for specific preparation guidelines. We provide detailed shipping instructions upon project confirmation.

Do you offer discounts for academic institutions?

Yes, we offer special pricing for academic and non-profit research institutions. Please mention your institutional affiliation when requesting a quote.

How do I receive my results?

Results are typically delivered via secure digital download. For digital pathology services, you'll receive access to our secure viewing platform. Physical slides and materials can be returned upon request.

Can you help with study design and consultation?

Yes, our team of pathologists and scientists offers consultation services for study design, biomarker selection, and analysis planning. Please contact us to discuss your specific research needs.
