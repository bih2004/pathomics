Payment Terms

Our payment policies and procedures for Pathomics services

General Payment Terms

At Pathomics, we strive to provide clear and transparent payment terms for all our services. Please review the following information regarding our payment policies and procedures.

Quotations and Pricing

  • All services require a formal quotation before work begins.
  • Quotations are valid for 30 days from the date of issue.
  • Pricing is based on the specific requirements of each project, including sample volume, complexity, and turnaround time.
  • Special pricing is available for academic institutions and non-profit organizations.
  • Volume discounts are available for large projects or standing orders.

Payment Methods

  • We accept payment by credit card, wire transfer, and institutional purchase orders.
  • Credit card payments can be made securely through our online payment portal.
  • Wire transfer details will be provided on invoices.
  • Purchase orders from established institutions are accepted subject to approval.
  • All payments must be made in US Dollars (USD).

Payment Schedule

  • For new clients, a 50% deposit is required before work begins, with the remaining balance due upon completion.
  • For established clients, payment terms are net 30 days from the invoice date.
  • For large projects (exceeding $10,000), a customized payment schedule may be arranged.
  • Rush services require payment in full before work begins.
  • Standing orders may qualify for monthly billing arrangements.

Invoicing

  • Invoices are issued electronically upon completion of services.
  • All invoices include detailed line items for services rendered.
  • Invoices can be customized to include purchase order numbers or other reference information.
  • Questions regarding invoices should be directed to our accounting department at accounting@pathomics.com.

Service-Specific Terms

Routine Services

Routine services include histology, basic immunohistochemistry, and digital slide scanning.

  • Standard payment terms apply as outlined above.
  • Rush processing (24-48 hour turnaround) is available for an additional 50% fee.
  • Same-day processing (when available) incurs a 100% rush fee.

Advanced Services

Advanced services include multiplex immunofluorescence, tissue microarray construction, and imaging analysis.

  • A 50% deposit is required for all advanced services regardless of client status.
  • Project-specific milestones and payment schedules may be established for complex projects.
  • Consultation fees may apply for experimental design and protocol development.

Custom and Research Services

Custom and research services include biomarker discovery, AI-powered pathology, and antibody validation.

  • Custom payment schedules are established based on project milestones.
  • Research collaboration agreements may include specialized payment terms.
  • Feasibility studies may be required before full project commitment.

Additional Policies

Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellation before work begins: Full refund of any deposits paid.
  • Cancellation after work begins but before completion: Payment required for work completed plus 25% of remaining project cost.
  • Rush service cancellations: Rush fees are non-refundable once work has been scheduled.
  • Cancellation requests must be submitted in writing to info@pathomics.com.

Sample Retention and Return

  • Unused sample materials are retained for 30 days after project completion unless otherwise specified.
  • Return shipping of samples and materials is available for an additional fee.
  • Long-term storage of samples or blocks can be arranged for a monthly fee.

Confidentiality and Intellectual Property

  • All client information and project details are kept strictly confidential.
  • Intellectual property rights remain with the client unless otherwise specified in a separate agreement.
  • Non-disclosure agreements are available upon request.

Late Payment Policy

  • Payments received after the due date are subject to a 1.5% monthly late fee.
  • Accounts more than 60 days past due may be referred to collections.
  • Future services may require prepayment for accounts with payment history issues.

Special Arrangements

Academic and Non-Profit Organizations

We offer special pricing and payment terms for academic institutions and non-profit organizations. Please contact our sales team for more information.

Volume Discounts

Clients with high-volume or recurring projects may qualify for volume discounts and specialized payment arrangements. Please inquire about our volume pricing options.

Research Collaborations

We welcome research collaborations with academic and industry partners. Collaborative projects may include custom payment terms, co-authorship arrangements, or other considerations.
